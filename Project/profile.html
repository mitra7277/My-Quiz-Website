My favourite Company Name Below Section

Tesla

Tesla, Inc. is a multinational company specializing in electric vehicles (EVs), renewable energy solutions, and energy storage systems Founded in 2003 by engineers Martin Eberhar and Marc Tarpenning, the company was later significantly shaped by Elon Musk, who joined as an investor and chairman in 2004. Musk is now the face of the company and its CEO.

Amazon

Amazon.com, Inc. is a multinational technology and e-commerce company that has grown to dominate global retail, cloud computing, and various other industries. It was founded on July 5, 1994, by Jeff Bezos in Seattle, Washington. Initially, Amazon started as an online bookstore but quickly expanded its offerings to become "The Everything Store."

Zomato

Zomato is an Indian multinational restaurant discovery and food delivery platform that connects users with restaurants, food delivery services, and dining experiences. It was founded in 2008 by Deepinder Goyal and Pankaj Chaddah in Gurgaon, India. Initially called Foodiebay, it rebranded to Zomato in 2010 and has since expanded its services globally.

Instagram

Instagram is a globally popular photo and video-sharing social media platform known for its focus on visual content. It was launched on October 6, 2010, by Kevin Systrom and Mike Krieger and was acquired by Facebook (now Meta Platforms, Inc.) in 2012.
